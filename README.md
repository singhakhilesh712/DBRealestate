# DBRealestate 0.1
Real Estate MYSQL Project

Introduction :
Real estate is land and the buildings on it, like houses, stores, and offices. People need real estate to have a place to live, work, and do business. There are four main types: homes where people live, stores and offices where people work, factories where things are made, and empty land that can be used for farming or building. Some people buy real estate to live in, while others buy it to rent or sell later for more money. The price of real estate changes depending on where it is and how many people want it. It’s a way people can make money by owning property.


Objectives :
Increase the property's worth through location, quality, and design.
Develop properties that attract buyers or renters for profitability.
Track properties, agents, clients, transactions, and visits effectively.

Agent Performance Analysis – Identify top-performing agents by tracking the number of sales and total revenue. This helps recognize the best agents and optimize sales strategies.
Property Demand Trends – Analyse which property types and locations are most in demand. This allows better targeting of listings and marketing efforts.
Client Engagement & Conversion Rate – Track how many property visits lead to actual transactions. Understanding client behaviour improves lead conversion strategies.
Sales and Revenue Trends – Monitor monthly and yearly sales growth from completed transactions. This helps in forecasting revenue and setting financial goals.
Pricing Insights – Compare listing prices with final sale prices to identify pricing trends. Helps in setting competitive prices to maximize profitability.
